One pseudotime axis spans three stages — zygote → early-2-cell → late-2-cell. Zygotes are ordered by the pronuclei-distance clock; 2-cell embryos have no pronuclei, so within their stage they are ordered by total transcript count (a maturation proxy). For zygotes the 3-D view shows the two pronuclei + min-distance line.
