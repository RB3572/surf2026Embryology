Training & validation summary

A geometric clock that estimates normalized pronuclear-migration pseudotime τ for a single fixed zygote from geometry alone. τ = (time since pronuclear formation) / (pronuclear formation → NEBD), reported on [0, 1]. Trained on public live-imaged control zygotes, validated with embryo-grouped cross-validation, then frozen and applied to this project's fixed MERFISH zygotes.

What pseudotime noise does to transcript R²

The clock has a measured error. Any transcript-versus-pseudotime fit is therefore attenuated: a real biological trend shows up weaker than it truly is, because the x-axis itself is noisy. This section quantifies that attenuation, so an observed R² can be interpreted rather than dismissed — the analysis requested in the 23 Jul meeting.
